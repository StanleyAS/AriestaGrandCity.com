About Us

As one of shopping and entertainment centers in the liveliest area in Bekasi,
Ariesta Grand City was initially known as the Ariesta mall and established since 2003 on a site of 25 Ha.
In February 2012, Ariesta Grand City is officially open as the world class shopping mall sitting on a site of 50.000 m²
designed by an famous european Architect for its luxurious design.

Strategically located with an easy access, Ariesta Grand City is providing one stop place to serve modern life
including shopping center, variety of dining options, and entertainment for family.
Ariesta Grand City also provides Convention Center, Ballroom, and multifunction Atrium to host various events
such as concert, promotion, live performance, or the company gathering.

Ariesta Grand City is expanding to be the most luxurious mall in bekasi offering one stop living concept
comprise mall complex, luxurious Apartment, and several leading hotels.
This expansion facilitates the needs of modern society lifestyles that continue to grow
as well as PT ariesta star commitment to maintain quality as our main priority in this industry.

Consisted of famous retail tenants such as apple store, Batik keris, optik melawai, levi’s, H&M, 3Second,
Matahari Department Store, famers market, Gramedia, toys kingdom, informa, Starbucks, CGV cinema
and more than 300 other tenants, Ariesta Grand City are perfect destinations
for your recreational and shopping needs.
